Annie Edwards is a professionally trained physical planner and environmental management specialist with over 20 years of experience in land use planning. Mrs. Edwards has a Dip in Physical Planning Technology and a BSc in Information Systems and Management. She also holds MSc in Natural Resource and Environmental Management and MSc in Project Management. A former Chief Physical Planner, she currently performs the duties of Physical Planner and Senior Project Co-ordinator for the ongoing National Land Use Policy and National Physical Development Plan Project, funded by the Caribbean Development Bank.
